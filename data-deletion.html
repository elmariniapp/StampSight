Delete your data, clearly.

This page explains how users of StampSight can delete local data stored on their device and request deletion of eligible backend-supported verification data.

Quick summary

Most StampSight data is stored locally on your device and can usually be removed directly by deleting proofs, exports, app data, or by uninstalling the app.

Important:

If you used QR verification or related verification features, some limited verification-related records may also exist on backend services and can be reviewed for deletion on request.

1 · Local data on your device

What is stored locally

StampSight is designed with significant local-first behavior. Many records are stored directly on your device to support proof capture, organization, and export workflows.

Examples of local data

Proof records, captured images, PDF exports, derived export images, client/site/mission data, app preferences, and local history records.

How users can remove it

Delete proofs or exports inside the app where available, clear app data from device settings, or uninstall the app.

Most deletion actions are immediate on-device.

Because the app is local-first, many records can be removed directly by the user without waiting for a support request.

2 · Backend-supported verification data

What may exist outside the device

If you use StampSight verification features, limited verification-related records may be processed through backend services used to support QR verification and proof validation workflows.

  • verification identifiers
  • proof identifiers
  • timestamps
  • integrity-related hashes
  • technical records required to operate the verification feature
Limited public display only.

Public verification pages intentionally display only a limited subset of proof-related information. Original source photos and full exported PDF files are not publicly exposed through the verification page.

3 · How to request deletion

Request deletion in 3 simple steps

If you want to request deletion of eligible backend-supported verification data or need privacy assistance, contact us using the method below.

Send your request by email Contact support@elmariniapp.com with the subject: StampSight Data Deletion Request
Include identifying details Mention the app name StampSight, the reason for your request, and any proof or verification reference that helps identify the relevant record.
We review and process the request We will review the request within a reasonable time and process eligible deletion as soon as reasonably practical, subject to verification and legitimate retention needs.

4 · What may be deleted or retained

Deletion scope

Depending on the request and the data involved:

  • local data can usually be deleted directly by the user from the device
  • eligible backend-supported verification data may be deleted upon request
  • some limited data may be retained where reasonably necessary for legal obligations, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, troubleshooting, or service integrity

Usually deletable

Local proofs, local exports, app data on the device, and eligible backend-supported verification records when identified and applicable.

May be retained

Limited technical or compliance-related data where retention is reasonably necessary for security, abuse prevention, legal obligations, or service integrity.

5 · Retention and timing

How long it can take

We review deletion requests within a reasonable time. Where deletion applies, we process the request as soon as reasonably practical, subject to request verification, technical feasibility, and any legitimate retention needs described on this page.

If additional information is needed to identify the relevant record, we may ask for reasonable details necessary to understand and process the request.
